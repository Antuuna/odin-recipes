Smash Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 soft hamburger roll, buttered and toasted
  • Ketchup as desired
  • Mayonnaise as desired
  • Mustard as desired
  • Shredded lettuce as desired
  • Onion as desired
  • Tomatoes as desired
  • Pickles as desired
  • 4 oz (110g) freshly ground beef chuck, divided into two 2 oz (55g) balls
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 American or Cheddar cheese
