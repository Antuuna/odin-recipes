Sushi Bake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 Tbps. rice vinegar
  • 1 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Tsp. sugar
  • 1/2 lbs. imitation crab meat
  • 1 Tbsp. sriracha
  • 1/2 Tsp. wasabi
  • 1 1/2 oz. cream cheese
  • 2 green onions chopped
  • 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
  • Furikake seasoning to taste
  • Seawood snacks for serving
